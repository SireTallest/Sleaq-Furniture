About Us

Built in Nigeria.
Trusted Worldwide.

At Sleaq Furniture, we believe that great spaces are not built by chance — they are crafted with intention. From our workshop in Lagos, we design and build premium living room, bedroom, and office furniture that transforms houses into homes and offices into inspiring workspaces.

What started as a one-man passion for craftsmanship has grown into a brand trusted by clients across Nigeria and beyond — with customers calling in from abroad to bring Sleaq's quality back home.

Every piece is personally handled with care and trust, from design to final delivery. Every piece we create carries one promise — that when you live or work with Sleaq furniture, you feel it.
